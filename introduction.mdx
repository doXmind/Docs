## Welcome to doXmind

doXmind is the next-generation AI-native document platform that transforms how professionals work with documents and data. We're building the future of knowledge work, where AI doesn't just assist - it collaborates.

From intelligent writing assistance to processing millions of data points with multi-agent AI, doXmind is your complete AI companion for research, analysis, and document creation.

## Why doXmind?

### Unified AI-Native Workflow
Stop switching between ChatGPT, Excel, Google, and Word. Everything happens in one place - AI-powered formatting, embedded research, interactive charts and tables, all in your writing flow.

### Private-by-Default Security
Built for professionals who handle sensitive data. Local deployment options, enterprise SSO, complete activity logs, and offline capabilities. Perfect for finance, healthcare, and legal teams.

### True AI Co-Authoring
200K token context that remembers your entire project. Inline autocomplete, select-to-edit with visual diffs, and multi-turn refinement over weeks or months. This isn't a chatbot - this is AI as your collaborative writing partner.
