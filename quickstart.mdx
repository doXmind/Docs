## Get Beta Access

doXmind is currently in Open Beta and available for everyone to try.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request Access">
    Visit [www.doxmind.com](https://www.doxmind.com) and apply for beta access

    *Note: The waitlist application page is currently under development. Access may be granted directly during early beta phase.*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Confirmation">
    You'll receive an email with your beta access confirmation and next steps
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Platform">
    Navigate to [beta.doxmind.app](https://beta.doxmind.app) and sign in with your credentials
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quick Start Guide

### 1. Create Your First Workspace

After signing in, you'll need to create a workspace to get started:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'New Workspace'">
    From the main dashboard, click the "New Workspace" button
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Workspace">
    Give your workspace a descriptive name (e.g., "My Research Project", "Content Writing")
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Choose your workspace preferences and click "Create"
  </Step>
</Steps>

### 2. Start Working with doXmind Agent

Once your workspace is ready, interact with the doXmind AI agent through the sidebar:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ask Questions">
    Use the sidebar to ask doXmind questions about your work, research topics, or get AI assistance
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Create Documents">
    Request doXmind to help you create, edit, and enhance documents
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Analyze Data">
    Upload data and ask doXmind to analyze, visualize, or extract insights
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Core Features

### AutoComplete
Start typing and doXmind will intelligently suggest completions based on your context and knowledge bases.

### Knowledge Bases
Build and manage your own knowledge repositories that doXmind can reference when assisting you with tasks.

### Document Editing
Collaborative document editing powered by AI. Create, refine, and perfect your documents with intelligent assistance.

### AI Collaboration
Work alongside doXmind's AI agent to:
- Brainstorm ideas
- Research topics
- Draft and refine content
- Solve complex problems

### Document Analysis
Upload and analyze documents to extract key insights, summaries, and actionable information.

### Data Analysis & Visualization
Transform raw data into meaningful insights with:
- Automated data analysis
- Interactive visualizations
- Statistical summaries
- Custom charts and graphs
